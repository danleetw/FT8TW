About Psk<BR>
<BR>
This feature allows you to upload received signals to the PskReporter website, <BR>
making it easier for you or others to observe overall communication propagation conditions.<BR>
<BR>
You need to specify your own callsign, location, and the antenna you are using.<BR>
<BR>
You can enter your callsign on the website below to check communication details related to the callsign.
