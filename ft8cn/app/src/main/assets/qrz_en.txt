About QRZ

This feature allows you to upload the QSO record to QRZ immediately after QSO is completed.

You need to obtain the API KEY in the logbook of the QRZ and enter it into the software.

<B>Please note: QRZ only allows paid users to upload via the API key.<br>

If you do not have a paid subscription, there's no need to waste your time.</b>
